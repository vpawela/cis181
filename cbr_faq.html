Chicago Bear Rescue FAQs

What is the mission of Chicago Bear Rescue?
Chicago Bear Rescue is committed to rescuing and rehabilitating bears in need, providing them with proper care, and working towards their eventual release into suitable habitats.
How can I adopt a rescued bear?
We do not facilitate the adoption of bears as pets. Our goal is to reintroduce rehabilitated bears into the wild or place them in reputable wildlife sanctuaries where they can live in a natural environment.
Can I visit the bear rescue facility?
Currently, our facilities are not open to the public for safety and security reasons. However, we periodically organize educational events and fundraisers. Stay tuned to our website for updates on these events.
How are bears rescued in urban areas like Chicago?
We work closely with local authorities, wildlife experts, and concerned citizens to identify bears in distress. Trained professionals are then dispatched to safely rescue and transport the bears to our rehabilitation facilities.
How can I report a bear in distress?
If you encounter a bear in distress in the Chicago area, please contact our emergency hotline at [emergency number]. Do not attempt to approach or handle the bear yourself, as it may pose risks to both you and the bear.
